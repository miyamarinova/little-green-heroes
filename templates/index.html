About Us

Dear Friends, We are Martin and Maria! Our green family business was born in 2025, after years of dreams and shared love. We began grow microgreens for our family and friends as a hobby. Now, we are proud that we provide South-East Bulgaria with fresh, local, and delicious microgreens year round. Our small greenhouse farm is located in Roza, Yambol municipality. You are more than welcome to visit, meet and talk with us!
